Standard Relationship Set

TERMS

Participant
The subject of the survey.

Respondent
The person providing feedback about the participant.

Anonymity Threshold
The number of responses the participant must receive for this relationship before seeing scores for it on reports. The anonymity threshold is typically set to 3 for all relationships, except for manager and self, which are set to 1.

Recommended Number
The number of respondents suggested for this relationship. Ensure the number coincides with the anonymity threshold and is reasonable given the actual number of respondents with this relationship in your organization.

Relationship Abbreviation Anonymity Threshold Recommended Number Description
Self Self 1 1 The participant.
Manager Mgt 1 1 The person to whom the participant directly reports.
Peer Peer 3 4 People, generally at the participant’s organizational level, with whom they work (may include team members).
Direct Report D R 3 4 The participant’s subordinates.
Internal Customer IC 3 4 ther associates within the participant’s organization to whom they provide a product or service.
External Customer EC 3 4 People outside the participant’s organization to whom they provide a product or service.
Other Oth 3 4 Anyone not described by the above categories with whom the participant works (may be internal or external to their organization).
