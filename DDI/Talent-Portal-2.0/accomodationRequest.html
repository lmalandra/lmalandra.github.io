Requesting an Accommodation

Requests for accommodation are evaluated on a case-by-case basis to determine the appropriate course of action. To complete your accommodation request, please follow these steps:

  • Contact your process administrator to request an accommodation.
  • You will be sent an accommodation request form to complete and return.
  • You will need to submit written documentation for your accommodation that:
    • Is from a licensed or certified professional (please ensure the documentation contains the professionalâ€™s phone number to allow us to contact him/her)
    • Explains why you need an accommodation, and
    • Explains in detail the type of accommodation you require.

As soon as all information is received, the organization will review your request.

You will be contacted after the review is complete.
