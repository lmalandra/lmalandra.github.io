EUAL Tables - Other

Capitation Payments Table (EUAL_CAP)
The Capitation Payments table contains payments at the card holder/individual level made to Providers for a period of time.

Group Level Adjustments Table (EUAL_ADJ)
The Claim Adjustments table contains expenses and adjustments at the Group level for claims that are paid in bulk due to agreements between the participating and control plans. Drug rebates are also included in this table.

Member Level Cost Rate Adjustments Table (EUAL_CRA)>
The Cost Rate Adjustment table contains one row per a claim adjustment to a facility, which is the difference between the original payments paid by Highmark and the final audited payment amount. These adjustments are for claims that have already been processed, and can be credits or debits due depending on changes in reimbursement rates between Highmark and contracted facilities.
