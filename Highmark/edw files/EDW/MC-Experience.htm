Code Standardizations
The EDW converts the codes it receives to standardized values. This report shows the mapping of source system codes to standard EDW codes.

Global Transformations
Global Transformations contain logic that is applied to claims from multiple, or all, source systems. They are often referenced by the field mappings as TRxxx, where xx is a two-digit number.
