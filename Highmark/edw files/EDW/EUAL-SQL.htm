EUAL Usage - SQL Samples

The EUAL has eliminated the need for many of the typical Claim selection criteria based on the fact that only Adjudicated Claims with Original, Adjusted and Voided Claim lines are loaded. Some other common selection criteria may be required to limit the result set to certain claim types (Inpatient, Outpatient, etc), or for certain date ranges based upon the Business Content Load Date.

The following basic sample queries illustrate how to develop a query to extract Inpatient, Outpatient, Professional, or Drug claims from the EUAL, and also include the resulting values of Claim and Claim line data elements retrieved from EUAL.
