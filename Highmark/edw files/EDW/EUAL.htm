Financial Equations
The Medical Experience Financial Equation incorporates the various dollar amounts that may apply to a claim line item or overall claim throughout the claim’s lifecycle – from submission through posting to the General Ledger. Most claims are represented by a general financial equation, however there are two cases that utilize separate, unique equations – one for Complementary and one for Medco. The document above contains a summary of the components that make up the Medical Experience Financial Equation, including definitions for each component.
